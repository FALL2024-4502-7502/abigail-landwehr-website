Abigail Landwehr's Site

Abigail Landwehr is a multimedia journalist. This means that her interest ranges from anything the broad term covers: writing, photography, videography, design, and audio. She just loves the storytelling process the most.

She grew up in Wyoming, about an hour and a half away from Yellowstone- that also means enough “Wyoming doesn’t exist” jokes to last her a lifetime. In her free time, she enjoys exactly the same things she does in her daily routine including storytelling, carrying her camera around wherever she goes and making obscure 90s sitcom references. (Ask her about Frasier, she loves Frasier.)
